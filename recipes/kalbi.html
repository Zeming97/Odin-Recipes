Kalbi

World's Best Kalbi

Kalbi is a staple of Korean fine dining but is too expensive in restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Stir sauce together.
  2. Place ribs in resealable bag.
  3. Preheat outdoor grill.
  4. Remove excess marinade and grill.
